Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Vendo collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services and improve your experience. This includes:

  • Personal identification: Name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address.
  • Payment details: Credit/debit card information (processed securely by our payment partners).
  • Account credentials: Username and password (hashed and encrypted).
  • Usage data: Browsing history, product views, search queries, and interactions.
  • Device & technical: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
We never store your full payment information on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Order processing: To fulfill and deliver your purchases.
  • Account management: To maintain your profile and preferences.
  • Customer support: To respond to inquiries and resolve issues.
  • Personalization: To recommend products and tailor your shopping experience.
  • Marketing: To send you promotional offers (with your consent).
  • Improvement: To analyze trends and enhance our services.
You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, analyze traffic, and personalize content.

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site operations (e.g., shopping cart, login).
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness.
  • Preferences: Remember your language and region settings.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Payment processors, shipping carriers, and IT support.
  • Business partners: Brands and sellers who fulfill your orders.
  • Legal compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
All third-party partners are contractually obligated to protect your data.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to safeguard your personal information:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission.
  • Access controls: Strict internal access policies.
  • Regular audits: Periodic security reviews and vulnerability testing.
  • Data minimization: We only collect what is necessary.

While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication where available.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Opt-out of certain processing activities (e.g., marketing).
To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@vendo.com

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account.
  • Transaction history: Kept for at least 7 years for tax and accounting purposes.
  • Marketing preferences: Retained until you unsubscribe.

You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Vendo is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it promptly. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact us.

9. International Data Transfers

Vendo is based in the United States. If you are accessing our services from outside the US, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US.

We take appropriate safeguards to ensure that your data is protected in accordance with this policy, including standard contractual clauses and adherence to applicable data protection laws.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the effective date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or through a prominent notice on our site.

Effective date: June 16, 2026
