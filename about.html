About Vendo

We're on a mission to create a better shopping experience — one that's thoughtful, sustainable, and built for you.

Our story

Vendo was born from a simple idea: shopping should feel personal, not transactional. Founded in 2020, we set out to build a marketplace that connects people with products they truly love.

We partner with independent makers, sustainable brands, and trusted suppliers to bring you a curated selection that's as unique as you are. Every item tells a story — from the artisan who crafted it to the journey it takes to reach your door.

100% independent & founder-led

What we stand for

Curated with care

Every product is handpicked for quality, style, and purpose. We don't just sell — we curate.

Sustainable choices

We prioritize eco-friendly materials, ethical production, and packaging that respects the planet.

Community first

We're built around people — our customers, creators, and team. Your voice shapes everything we do.

Fair & transparent

No hidden fees, no gimmicks. We believe in honest pricing and straightforward policies.
